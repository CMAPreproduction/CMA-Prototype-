Send funeral or crematorium information to the CMA

Funeral directors and crematorium operators can use this service to submit revenue returns and compliance statements

Information you will need

You only need to submit information if you are:

  • A crematorium operator
  • A funeral director operating 5 or more branches

In completing this form, you will be asked to provide information including:

  • your name and business details
  • data on your business’ revenue
  • number of funerals or cremations your business carried out during the reporting period
  • depending on your business, you may also be asked to submit a compliance statement (for all crematorium operators and funeral directors with 10 or more branches)

Compliance statements for funeral directors must be submitted by a Chief Executive Officer, Managing Director, Non-Executive Director, Senior Director, or a business owner.

By filling out this form, you confirm you are a decision maker at your business and can verify the compliance statement.

Crematorium operators also need to submit a compliance statement.

You’ll need all your information with you, as you’re unable to save this information or come back to it later.
