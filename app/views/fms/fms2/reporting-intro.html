Alternative Reporting Approach

Rather than present a user with a menu of choices of what they are reporting as a series of check-boxes, a series of questions would interactively determine the appropriate forms to show.

The questions ask:

  • Organisation Type - Funeral Director, Crematorium Operator, or both
  • Number of Branches - shown if Funeral Director chosen - more than 5, or more than 10
  • Reporting Period - shown if Crematorium Operator chosen - April and/or October
  • Compliance Statement - asks if they will be filing a compliance statement

For the purposes of this prototype, these questions are NOT responsive to each other, but in a real application, they would be (so for example the branches question is only asked if you prevously chose "Funeral Director").

After answering the above questions, the user would be led through the appropriate forms to satisfy their answers (parts A, B, C and D of the submission, and an appropriate compliance statement).
