Funerals compliance form

Use this service to tell the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) about your pricing and revenue returns or compliance statements as a Funeral Director, Crematorium Operator (or both).

The information you provide will help your business comply with Articles 9, 10 and 11 of the Funerals Market Investigation Order 2021.

Information you will need to provide

You will be asked to provide your business':

  • Name and business details
  • Data on revenue
  • Number of funerals / cremations carried out
  • Compliance statements

Compliance statements for a corporate funeral director business must be submitted by a Chief Executive Officer, Managing Director or any Non-Executive Director or any Senior Director of a Funeral or Crematorium business.

Compliance statements for a sole trader or partnership funeral director must be submitted by the owners of the business or by individuals with decision-making authorities.

Before you start

The information you share with us and your 'personal data' will only be used by the CMA to help businesses comply with the Funerals Order.

'Personal data' is information which relates to an individual who can be identified from it, which will be handled in line with our personal information charter (also known as privacy notice).
