Crematorium operators returns (April)

Please add the total revenue and total number of cremations carried out by your business in each of the services set out below.

This should include all revenue generated from crematoria services excluding burial services.

Please do not include revenue from memorials related to crematoria services here, this can be provided in the next step.

These should be within the first quarter period from 1 March (6 months) and within the second quarter period from 1 June (6 months).

Leave the boxes blank if not applicable to your business. Once all your information is entered, select Continue.
