Send funeral or crematorium statements to the CMA

Use this service to submit revenue returns and compliance statements, in line with the requirements of the Funerals Market Investigation Order 2021.

Funeral directors and crematorium operators are required to fill out this form twice a year, covering the periods of 1 March to 31 August and 1 September to end of February.

Information we will ask for

In completing this form, you will be asked to provide information including:

  • Your name and business details
  • Data on your business' revenue
  • Number of funerals or cremations your business carried out during the reporting period
  • Compliance statement
