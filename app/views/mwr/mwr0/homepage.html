CMA clears Sony’s acquisition of AWAL

16 March 2022 — Press release

The CMA has cleared Sony’s acquisition of AWAL, an ‘artist and label’ (A&L) services provider, following an in-depth merger inquiry.

CMA finds NortonLifeLock purchase of Avast could reduce competition

16 March 2022 — Press release

The CMA has found that NortonLifeLock’s approximately £6bn purchase of Avast raises competition concerns and may now be referred for an in-depth investigation.

CMA launches investigation into recycling of cars and vans

15 March 2022 — Press release

The CMA has launched an investigation into suspected anti-competitive conduct in relation to the recycling of old or written-off vehicles.
