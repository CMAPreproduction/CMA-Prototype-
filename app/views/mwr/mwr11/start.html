Make a whistleblower report

Use this service to tell the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) about any suspected illegal activities or suspicious behaviour carried out by the business or organisation you're employed by or were previously employed by.

As part of your report, you can choose to provide contact details to us or remain anonymous.

The CMA won't respond directly to every report we receive, however we monitor information provided and can take action where appropriate.

Find out more about whistleblowing, and what it means to be a whistle-blower, including your rights and how we will protect your identity.

Information you will need to provide

You will not be able to save your submission and come back to it later, so it's important you have these ready:

  • A description of the issue you're reporting
  • Supporting evidence, if you have any
