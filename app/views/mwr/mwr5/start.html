Make a Whistleblower Report

Use this service to tell the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) about illegal activities carried out by the business or organisation you're employed by.

If the illegal activity relates to the sale of products, the supply of services or about competition affecting markets in the UK, it's important you report it to the CMA.

Find out more about whistleblowing, and what it means to be a whistle-blower, including your rights and how the CMA will protect your identity.

The information you provide will help the CMA to prioritise its action against possible breaches of competition or consumer law.

Information you will need to provide

You will be asked to provide:

  • A description of the issue, including its impact
  • Any supporting evidence you have
  • Details about yourself and, if applicable, your organisation
