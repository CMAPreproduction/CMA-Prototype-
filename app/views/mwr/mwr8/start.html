Make a Whistleblower report

Use this service to tell the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) about illegal activities carried out by the business or organisation you're employed by or were previously employed by.

As part of your report, you can choose to provide contact details to the CMA or remain anonymous.

Find out more about whistleblowing, and what it means to be a whistle-blower, including your rights and how the CMA will protect your identity.

Information you will need to provide

You will be asked to provide:

  • A description of the issue, including its impact
  • Any supporting evidence you have
  • Details about yourself and, if applicable, your organisation

The CMA will not respond directly to every report it receives, however it monitors information provided and will take action where appropriate.
